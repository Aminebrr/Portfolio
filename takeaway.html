01 — The Brief

The problem we solved.

Takeaway dominated Benelux market share but faced stagnating repeat-order rates and rising CAC in France and Germany. The brand felt transactional, not emotional.

Business Challenge

Repeat order rate had plateaued at 38% — below the EU average of 46%. New user acquisition costs rose 34% YoY, making retention the core growth lever.

Brand Challenge

— functional but forgettable. Uber Eats et Deliveroo gagnaient en affinité de marque auprès des 18–34 ans."> Takeaway was perceived as "the cheaper option" — functional but forgettable. Competitors like Uber Eats and Deliveroo were winning on brand affinity with 18–34s.

Communication Objective

Build emotional resonance around the concept of craving — positioning Takeaway as the brand that understands hunger on a deeply personal level, not just a delivery platform.

Target Audience

Urban millennials & Gen Z (18–34) across 4 markets. Secondary: busy professionals (28–42). Both over-index on late-night orders, social moments, and personalization.

02 — Strategic Framework

The 3-pillar strategy.

Built around a single human truth: craving is irrational, urgent, and deeply personal. The campaign had to reflect that energy across every touchpoint.

1
🧠
Crave Capture

Intercept users at the peak moment of craving — not just mealtimes. Real-time contextual targeting via weather API, time-of-day, and live events across paid social and programmatic.

2
🔁
Loop Lock

Transform first orders into habits. A gamified loyalty mechanic — Crave Credits — rewarded streak ordering, referrals, and reviews, deepening the behavioral loop beyond simple discounts.

3
📣
Culture Injection

Embed the brand into cultural conversation. Micro-creator seeding + meme culture made 'the craving' a shared, shareable feeling — generating organic reach on TikTok and Instagram.

03 — Campaign Execution

12-week rollout plan.

01
Weeks 1–3 · Ignition
Teaser & Brand Heat

Cryptic 'What are you craving?' OOH campaign in Paris, Amsterdam, Berlin & Brussels — QR codes leading to a craving personality quiz. Zero branding. Generated 180K quiz completions and 2.4M impressions before the reveal. 60 micro-creators seeded with mystery hampers for unboxing content.

OOHTeaserInfluencerQuiz Funnel
02
Weeks 4–7 · Amplify
Big Brand Moment + Paid Blitz

Live reveal on TikTok & Instagram: 'We Are Takeaway. We Know Your Craving.' Simultaneous paid media activation across Meta, TikTok Ads, YouTube Pre-roll & Spotify. Dynamic creative optimization (240+ ad variants) based on time-of-day, weather, and user data. Crave Credits loyalty programme launched with a 2× bonus window.

Live EventPaid SocialDCOLoyalty Launch
03
Weeks 8–10 · Sustain
Community & Culture Play

#CraveItChallenge on TikTok — users shared their craving moment for a chance to win a year of free orders. 8 macro-influencer integrations. CRM email & push using personalized order history ('Last time you ordered this at 11pm. Tonight?'). Retargeting of quiz completers with conversion creatives.

04
Weeks 11–12 · Convert
Performance Sprint & Retention Close

Flash 'Crave Drops' — 48h discounts unlocked via social share. Re-engaged lapsed users with a 'We missed your cravings' email sequence. Published a shareable 'Cravings Wrapped' personalization moment — shared 340K times across markets. Closed with loyalty milestone rewards.
